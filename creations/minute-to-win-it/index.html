Race against the clock in a collection of fast-paced 60-second Minecraft challenges inspired by Minute to Win It.

Players must complete each game before time runs out. Every challenge tests a different skill, including speed, accuracy, movement, timing, memory, and coordination. Some games are simple to learn but difficult to master, especially when only one minute stands between you and elimination.

Play by yourself, challenge friends, or host a full competition with multiple contestants. Keep track of completed games, eliminate players after failed challenges, and continue until only one competitor remains.

The map is designed for exciting multiplayer events, videos, livestreams, and game nights.

Complete the challenges. Beat the clock. Become the last player standing.
