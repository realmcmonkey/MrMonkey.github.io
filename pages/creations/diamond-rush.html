Diamond Rush

Diamond Rush is an intense minigame where you have to mine as many diamond ores as you can and then smelt them to get diamonds. All of this has to be done during a period of just two minutes. If you collect 32 diamonds then you'll win the game. This game can be played alone or with multiple players. It's based off another map here on MCPE DL called Gold Rush.

How to play?

All you have to do is collect 32 diamonds and smelt them and you will win the games. You have 2 minutes. This map can be played with as many players as you want.
